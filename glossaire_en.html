Glossary

Rape culture
Social concept gathering all actions and thinks which minimize, normalize even encourage rape.
Feminism
All actions, ideas, movement demanding equality between men and women.
Intersectional feminism
Feminism which take into account all forms of discrimination that women can suffer from, in their individuality.
Law Veil
Law decriminalizing abortion in France, prepared by Simone Veil and adopted on the 17th of january 1975.
Suffragettes
Women who campaigned for the right of vote. First, from the United Kingdom, the movement spread next in Europe.
Women’s lib
Abbreviation of Women’s Liberation Movement, american movement protesting against sexism and discriminations and demanding rights equality between me and women.
